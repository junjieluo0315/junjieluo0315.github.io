About Me

I'm a Technical Artist and Synthetic Data Engineer at ByteDance's Intelligent Creation Team, where I've been working since 2020. My core focus is developing Houdini 3D-based procedural rendering pipelines to generate customized training data.

The synthetic data I create supports video generation models and various computer vision tasks, playing a critical role in optimizing model performance by providing high-quality, diverse datasets.
